DESCRIPTION


In Taguig, SASHS refers to the Stand Alone Senior High School within Taguig Integrated School (TIS). It is a separate Senior High School (SHS) unit within TIS, with its own distinct administration and leadership. Essentially, it's a senior high school that operates independently from the junior high school and elementary levels within the larger Taguig Integrated School complex.
